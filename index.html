About Us

THE TWO SCISSORS SALON DIFFERENCE



Our carefully chosen and highly trained staff is here to exceed your expectations. The same goes for our elegant salon space – dedicated to guest comfort – and our devotion to innovation and artistry. We’re focused on making your day and continually raising the bar.

Our committed group of beauticians, nails and skin specialists accompany innumerable long periods of involvement in the magnificence business. We endeavor to make each customer our need, ensuring we tune in to everything about that we feature every individual’s common magnificence.
